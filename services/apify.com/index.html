Before you can set up 2-Step Verification for your Apify account you'll need to download Apify Authenticator App on your device by searching for it in your Apple device's App Store, click on the button below Download on the App Store or scan the QR Code.

How to recover your Apify account?

If you have lost access to your two-factor authentication (2FA) code generator, please get in touch with the Apify support team for assistance.

What authenticator does Apify use?

Apify uses two-factor authentication (2FA) to enhance the security of your account. For 2FA, Apify supports the use of the Authenticator App by 2Stable, among other options. This app generates a time-sensitive code that you must enter, along with your password, when logging in to your Apify account. This added layer of security helps protect against unauthorized access.
