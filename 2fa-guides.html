Protect Your Online Accounts with Two Factor Authentication (2FA)

Learn about two-factor authentication (2FA), an extra layer of security that includes SMS, email, phone calls, hardware, and software.

Install Authenticator

The best way to manage all your 2FA accounts is to use the Authenticator app. It enables you to have a single mobile app for all your 2FA accounts and you can sync them across multiple devices. Install Authenticator on your device by searching for it in your device’s app store.

Important

If any sites prompt you to use Google Authenticator for two-factor authentication, note you can always substitute it with the Authenticator app. Although they work in similar ways, Authenticator is more feature-rich and allows for multi-device syncing, iCloud-backups, and have easier account recovery in case you lose your device or just want to switch to a new one.
