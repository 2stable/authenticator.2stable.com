2FA Guides

Protect your online accounts with Two Factor Authentication (2FA)

The best way to manage all your 2FA accounts is to use the Authenticator app. If any sites prompt you to use Google Authenticator for two-factor authentication, note you can always substitute it with the Authenticator app. Although they work in similar ways, Authenticator is more feature-rich and allows for multi-device syncing, iCloud-backups, and have easier account recovery in case you lose your device or just want to switch to a new one.
